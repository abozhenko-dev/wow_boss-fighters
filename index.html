ABOUT THE GAME

"Boss Fighters" is an asymmetric multiplayer game where VR player takes the role of the powerful boss-monster, while PC players team up against it, battling on the futuristic gladiators show.

Hop on to play-to-earn battles to win valuable cosmetic items, skins and other rewards, stored on blockchain and tradable on the marketplace.
